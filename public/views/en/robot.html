Instruction

General information about the trading algorithm.

The trading algorithm has previously been used and has proven itself in trading futures on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME). It has several advantages over both live traders and other trading algorithms. Its main advantages over competitors are:

  • Speed: As the trading algorithm instantly reacts to changing market conditions, it opens a position immediately after identifying the entry point.
  • Diversification: Trades different assets using proven algorithms. This makes it possible to distribute risks among several financial instruments and avoid significant losses.
  • Rationality: Unlike novice traders whose assumptions and conclusions against the backdrop of accepted patterns are subjective, the trading algorithm works most efficiently and objectively based on pre-determined rules.
  • Strict discipline: Discipline is maintained even in unstable market conditions by observing and executing trading rules.
  • Consistency: Provides a systematic approach as it always trades automatically in accordance with the algorithm. For investing, it does not use more than 10% of your account balance (within the allowable rates provided by the broker). The trading mode on different account types (DEMO & REAL) may differ, as for trading on DEMO accounts, it uses an investment size of at least $100.

When trading on a REAL account, the investment size will vary depending on the balance. For example, if your balance is $400 and the broker provides investment sizes of $10, $25, $50, $100, $250, $500, and $1,000, the robot will open a trade with an investment size of $25 as this size will correspond to the Money & Risk management rules followed by the trading algorithm.

Before launching the trading algorithm, make sure you have selected the correct account type: REAL/DEMO.

There are also differences in stopping the trading algorithm.

When trading on DEMO, trading is stopped after clicking the "STOP" button + 1 trade that will be executed within 15 minutes.

When trading on REAL, trading is stopped after clicking the "STOP" button + 10 trades that will be executed within 1 hour.
